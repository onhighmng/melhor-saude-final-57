## ğŸ¯ Key Concepts Explained

### **What are "Sessions"?**
- Individual therapy/consultation appointments
- Company purchases a total pool (e.g., 100 sessions)
- Distributed fairly among all employees

### **What are "Seats"?**
- Maximum employee accounts allowed
- Subscription limit (e.g., 50 seats = max 50 employees)
- Determines per-employee allocation

### **Why divide sessions by seats?**
- **Fair distribution**: Everyone gets equal access
- **Simple management**: No manual allocation needed
- **Transparent**: Employees see their quota upfront

### **Example Calculation:**
```
Company buys: 100 sessions
Company has: 50 seats
Each employee gets: 100 Ã· 50 = 2 sessions

If only 25 employees join:
They still get 2 sessions each
(remaining capacity for 25 more employees)
```
